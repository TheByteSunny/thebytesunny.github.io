Creating HL2:SB++ Addons

Making addons for HL2:SB++ is simple. This guide covers the basic workflow.

Step 1 - Create an Addon Folder

Navigate to your HL2:SB++ installation and open: 

hl2sbpp/addons/

Create a new folder for your addon. 

hl2sbpp/addons/MyAddon/

Step 2 - Recreate Required Game Folders

Inside your addon folder, recreate only the directories that your addon needs.

Example: if you are creating a Physgun skin, you may only need: 

MyAddon/
└── materials/
    └── ...

Examples of commonly used folders:

  • materials
  • models
  • sound
  • lua
  • maps
  • resource

Step 3 - Package Your Addon

When your addon is finished, package it as:

  • .zip
  • .vpk

Either format is acceptable.

Step 4 - Upload the Addon

Upload your addon somewhere online.

Popular options include:

  • MediaFire
  • Google Drive
  • Any other file host

Step 5 - Share It

Post your download link in the sb-addons channel on the Discord server.

Step 6 - Submit to the Workshop

Use Jarvis but better to submit your addon to the workshop.

Provide your addon link to Jarvis but better

Done!

Congratulations! Your addon is now ready to be downloaded and used by other players.
